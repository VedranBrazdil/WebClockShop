Service and Repairs

This company is not here to only sell new clocks but to repair and fix the old ones also. We have service team in each of our shops that will tend and fix your clocks any time you need. Sometimes all your clock needs is a little bit of oil or a new batteries. We hope you will visit one of our shops if you want to extend life of your clock by few years.

If you need more information you can call our phone or contact us by email.

If our service team can't fix your clock, don't worry we have team of highly skilled engineer that will fix mechanism of your clock in a day.

Or replace it with new one if this one is unsavable, but be patient.

Take care of your clock!
